MONKEY'S TRAIL

Monkey Trail is a 16.1 kilometer moderately trafficked loop trail located near Keithville, Louisiana that features beautiful wild flowers and is rated as moderate. The trail is primarily used for hiking, running, horses, and mountain biking and is accessible year-round. Dogs are also able to use this trail but must be kept on leash. The Monkey Trail is a series of loop trails throughout 800+ acres of mature hardwood forest.
